Location

Little Corn Island is a raw, unspoiled gem in the Caribbean, off the coast of Nicaragua. No cars. Incredible snorkeling. The occasional blackout. A true escape.

Yemaya Reefs is a small private luxury resort at the northern tip of the island with 18 beachfront rooms.

Morning yoga, snorkel boat tours, scuba diving, fantastic food, lazy beach days. Crush the latest romantasy "novel" or book on AI.

Planning Your Trip

The key flight to make is the plane from MGA to Big Corn — via La Costena — which departs daily at 7am CST and 2pm CST.

If enough people arrive at the same time, we can charter the plane to/from Big Corn privately for the same round trip fare, affording us considerable flexibility in our MGA arrival times. There are 12-seater planes and 36-seater plane options.

Please RSVP and coordinate with us before you book your flights
