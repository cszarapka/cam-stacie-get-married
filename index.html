Location

The Dordogne region, located in southwest France between the Loire Valley and the Pyrenees, is known for its rolling green hills, medieval villages, limestone cliffs, and slow-moving rivers.

Château La Carrière is a thoughtfully restored 17th-century castle set amidst 28 hectares of private parkland in the charming village of Saint-Cyprien.

Planning Your Trip

The key flight to make is the plane from Managua (MGA) to Big Corn — via La Costena — which departs daily at 7am CST and 2pm CST.

If enough people arrive at the same time, Yemaya will charter the plane to/from Big Corn privately for the same round trip fare, affording us considerable flexibility in our MGA arrival times. There are 12-seater planes and 36-seater plane options.

This flight does have strict baggage requirements, which you can learn about here: Baggage, and here: Children & Infants

Please RSVP and coordinate with us before you book your flights

Accommodation

Château La Carrière has limited (8) guest rooms which we are first holding for our wedding party. However, the charming village of Saint-Cyprien (Dordogne) and the surrounding valley offer plentiful Airbnbs, boutique inns, and countryside gîtes within a short drive of the château.
