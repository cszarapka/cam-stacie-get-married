THE WEDDING OF

MAY 30 - JUNE 6, 2026 | LITTLE CORN ISLAND, NICARAGUA

MAY 30 - JUNE 6, 2026 | LITTLE CORN ISLAND, NICARAGUA


Prepare yourself for an adventure.


We are in the final stages of planning our dream wedding and want to share just exactly what we’re cooking up — we’re aware this is shorter notice and a bit wild.


Please consider this a “soft RSVP.”

Little Corn Island is a raw, unspoiled gem in the Caribbean, off the coast of Nicaragua. No cars. Incredible snorkeling. The occasional blackout. A true escape.


You fly into Augusto Cesar Sandino International Airport (MGA) in Managua, Nicaragua (12h-17h). From there, we take a small plane to Big Corn Island (1h30m) and then private boats to Little Corn (1h).


The key flight to make is the plane from MGA to Big Corn — via La Costena — which departs daily at 7am CST and 2pm CST.


If enough people arrive at the same time, we can charter the plane privately for the same round trip fare, affording us considerable flexibility in our MGA arrival times. There are 12-seater planes and 36-seater plane options.


Once at Big Corn, private boats take us directly to our resort, Yemaya Reefs.


*Once you RSVP, we can provide the exact flights we are taking and recommend.
