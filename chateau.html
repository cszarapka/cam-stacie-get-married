Amenities

PARKING

The château has 9 parking stalls available to its guests which should cover us all. Please let us know if that is not the case. During the events all other guests will be taxi'ing or shuttling in.

FOOD

There are 2 kitchens, one in each wing, and they're both equipped with the basics for guest use. We will be stocking the kitchens with basics for simple and grazing style breakfasts (eggs, yogurt, toast, pastries, coffee, etc.). Lunches and dinners will be up to you, save for the events of course.

POOL

The pool looks AWESOME! The only important note is that it's only available to the guests staying in the château. There are towels available for us to use.

ADDITIONAL FEATURES

The château has many features: hot tub; cinema room; games room, small gym, private lake with a gazebo where massages can be organized; a 6-seater golf cart we can use to explore the grounds. It is not a staffed property but we are able to organize masseurs and additional chefs if desired, so just let us know what you're feeling!

BATHROOMS

The bathrooms are fully equipped with everything you need. Every room has its own private bathroom except for the Platanes and Lauriers, which share a bathroom.
