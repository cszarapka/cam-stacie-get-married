Q + A

WHAT IS THE DRESS CODE?

The Welcome Party is Creative Cocktail attire (bright colours), inspo here. The Wedding is French Garden Formal (floor-length dresses), inspo here. If you've got a tux you want to dust off, this is your occasion! On the Wedding Day, the bridesmaids will be wearing french blue dresses. Dordogne in July gets up to about 27°C — dark-coloured linens are okay.

CAN I BRING MY KIDS?

While we adore your kids, we are asking to keep the Wedding Day adults-only so everyone can relax and enjoy the celebration. We hope you understand and can arrange childcare for the day if you do plan to bring your little one.

DID THE SCHEDULE CHANGE?

Yes! You're not crazy. We didn't initially realize we could only host 2 events at the chateau and that check-in on the 28th was at 5pm (making a welcome party that day rather rushed).

WHAT IS THE PARKING SITUATION?

There is very limited parking at the chateau. Please plan to arrive by taxi for the events.

IS THERE A GIFT REGISTRY?

We've asked you to come to a remote region in France and YOU SAID YES! We couldn't imagine a greater gift. If you are feeling keen — and that's beyond generous of you — we would greatly appreciate a honeymoon contribution.
